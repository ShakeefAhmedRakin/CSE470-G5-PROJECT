Easy Steps to Enjoy Journey

Search Destination!

  • • Go to busbd Website: https://busbd.com.bd
  • • Select your location
  • • Select bus type
  • • Select the date of your journey

Select Ticket!

  • • Click the view seats option
  • • Select your seats
  • • Provide all the information
  • • Select the next option
  • • By clicking on the operator name/image, you can see the entire bus image

Pay and Enjoy your Journey!

  • • You can pay via SSL Commerz, Nagad and bKash
  • • After making all the payment procedures, you will get your ticket
